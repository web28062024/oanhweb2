Homework Assignments

Here are some assignments to help you practice and reinforce what you've learned in class:

Geography

1. Create a map of your local area and label important landmarks.

2. Write a report on the climate of a country of your choice.

History

1. Research and write an essay on a significant historical figure.

2. Create a timeline of major events in the 20th century.
